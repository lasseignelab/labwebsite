When getting started with data analysis, we recommend:

  1. Check the data science guides listed above for general information on your topic

  2. Google any errors you might encounter and first read and try solutions

  3. Leave a message on the #datascience 'UAB Labs' Slack channel as described in the 'Data Science Office Hours' guide

  4. Attend 'Data Science Office Hours' on Zoom or Slack as described in the guide above

  5. If you have tried each step above and still require assistance, you can email Dr. Lasseigne at bnp0001[@]uab.edu. She will confirm you have walked through the above steps and then help you figure out your next one

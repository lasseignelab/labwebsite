Research

The Lasseigne Lab uses genomic strategies and data to study complex and rare diseases with the goal of discovering biological signatures that might be used to improve patient care and provide insight into molecular processes contributing to disease. We currently have active collaborations in cancer, neurodegeneration, kidney, and rare disease projects.
